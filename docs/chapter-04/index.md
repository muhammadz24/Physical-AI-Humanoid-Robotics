# Chapter 4: Digital Twin Simulation

## Chapter Overview

Welcome to the world of digital twin simulation, where virtual robots enable rapid prototyping, safe testing, and large-scale training without the costs and risks of physical hardware. This chapter explores Gazebo and NVIDIA Isaac Simâ€”two powerful simulation environments that have become essential tools in modern robotics development.

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand the role of simulation in robotics development
- Master Gazebo for ROS 2 robot simulation
- Learn URDF and SDF for robot modeling
- Explore NVIDIA Isaac Sim for GPU-accelerated simulation
- Integrate simulated sensors and physics-based environments

**Prerequisites:**
- Chapter 3: ROS 2 Fundamentals (nodes, topics, services)
- Basic understanding of 3D coordinate systems
- Familiarity with XML/YAML file formats
- Python programming

**Estimated Reading Time:** 40-45 minutes
